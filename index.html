Introduction

What this policy covers

AppLock Pro is an app locking application that protects your apps using PIN, password, pattern, or biometric authentication. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights as a user.

Our Promise: All your sensitive data — PINs, locked app list, intruder photos — stays on your device only. We never upload it to any server.

Intruder Selfie Feature

Camera usage details
  • Front camera automatically captures a photo after multiple failed unlock attempts
  • Photo is saved only on your device in the app's private folder
  • Photos are visible only to you in the Security section of the app
  • Photos are never uploaded to any server
  • You can disable this feature anytime in Settings → Security
  • You can delete any intruder photo individually from the app
⚠️ Camera is only used for intruder selfie capture. It is never used to monitor you or record video.
